Amanda Sarah Brioche

Physical Scientist, U.S. Geological Survey

I am a mineral commodity specialist for the National Minerals Information Center (NMIC) at the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) headquarters in Reston, Virginia. My work focuses on conducting qualitative and quantitative research and analysis of mineral industry data, including production, consumption and trade. I author annual publications for domestic boron and peat industries, as well as summarize international news pertaining to these commodities. I also specialize in geographic infromation systems (GIS), and use these skills to create the maps for some of our internal publications, such as the Mineral Commodity Summaries, and participate in other GIS related projects.
